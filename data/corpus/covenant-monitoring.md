# Covenant Monitoring Guide

## Standard post-close triggers

The following thresholds should move a live account onto the watchlist:

- DSCR below 1.15x.
- Two consecutive months of negative net cash flow.
- Overdue receivables above 18% of the current book.
- Top customer concentration above 40%.
- Two or more returned payments or NSF events in any rolling 90-day window.

## Structural responses

When a watchlist trigger trips, respond with the lightest structure that still protects downside:

- Increase reserve by 3% to 5% before cutting the facility if collections remain orderly.
- Tighten borrowing-base eligibility if a single obligor is driving the deterioration.
- Require weekly reporting when liquidity is thinning faster than receivables are turning.

## Escalation threshold

Escalate from watchlist to remediation when multiple triggers stack at once. For example:

- DSCR below 1.00x plus repeated negative balance days.
- Rising overdue receivables plus shrinking liquidity runway.
